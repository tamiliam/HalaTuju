## 4. Basic Requirements
"You can apply if you…" (icon checklist):
- Are a **Malaysian of Indian descent** *(for this pilot — this may widen in
  future)*.
- Come from a **B40 household** — combined monthly income **below RM5,860**
  (DOSM 2024 B40 ceiling). Receiving **STR** or **JKM** is a strong signal.
- Have **at least 5 A's in SPM**, **or** a **PNGK of 3.0 or above in STPM**.
- Are **continuing your post-secondary studies at a public institution** —
  pre-university (Matrikulasi, Foundation/Asasi at an IPTA or polytechnic, STPM)
  or tertiary (IPTA, polytechnic / ILKA): e.g. matrikulasi, asasi, STPM, PISMP,
  diploma or degree.
- Are **willing to be contacted** about your application.

Small note: *"Close but not exactly? Apply anyway — we also consider students who
narrowly miss one criterion."* (Bucket B)

## 5. How It Works (Application → Award)
A simple numbered timeline. **No fixed deadline — applications are reviewed on a
rolling basis during the pilot.**

1. **Apply** — sign in with Google, confirm the details from your HalaTuju
   profile, and submit. About 5–10 minutes.
2. **We confirm** — you'll get an email confirming we've received your application.
3. **Shortlisting** — we check your results and need against the criteria, and let
   you know if you've been shortlisted.
4. **Complete your profile** — if shortlisted, finish your HalaTuju quiz (if you
   haven't), tell us more about your goals and needs, declare any other assistance
   you receive, and upload supporting documents (IC, results slip, proof of
   income/aid).
5. **A quick chat (sometimes)** — we may arrange a short phone call to get to know
   you.
6. **Ready for sponsors** — with your consent, we prepare a profile (your private
   details stay hidden) for sponsors to consider.
7. **Matched & awarded** — when a sponsor chooses to support you, we'll let you
   know and arrange the assistance. *(Subject to sponsor availability.)*
8. **Staying supported** — once awarded, you'll keep your study progress updated so
   support can continue. *(Coming with the sponsor programme.)*

Reassurance line: *"Most of this happens online, and we'll guide you at each step
by email and in the app."*

## 6. FAQ
**Do I need to have used HalaTuju before?**
No. If you're new, the same Google sign-in creates your account. If you've already
done the HalaTuju quiz, we'll use your profile so you don't retype anything.

**Who can apply?**
For this pilot, the programme is open to **Malaysian students of Indian descent**
from B40 households who meet the academic criteria. This scope may widen in future.

**Is this a loan? Do I have to pay it back?**
No. It is education **assistance from a sponsor** — nothing to repay, no interest.
[final wording subject to lawyer review]

**Is it guaranteed if I qualify?**
No. This is a pilot with limited places and strict partner criteria. Applying lets
us assess you; it doesn't guarantee assistance.

**Who can see my personal details?**
Your private details (IC, contact, exact address) are **never** shown to sponsors.
Sponsors only see a profile, and only after you consent. If you're under 18, a
parent or guardian must give that consent. Your answers stay with the coordinators
and partners involved in this pilot.

**What documents will I need?**
After you're shortlisted: your IC, SPM/STPM results slip, and proof of household
income or aid (e.g. STR, EPF statement). We'll tell you exactly what's needed.

**What levels does it cover?**
**Public post-secondary study** — both pre-university (Matrikulasi, Foundation/Asasi
at an IPTA or polytechnic, STPM) and tertiary (IPTA, polytechnic / ILKA).

**Does it cover private colleges (IPTS)?**
Not in this pilot — it supports study at **public** institutions only. Foundation or
Asasi must be at a public institution (IPTA or polytechnic).

**I already receive other help (PTPTN, JKM, biasiswa). Can I still apply?**
Yes. You don't need to list it to apply — if you're shortlisted, we'll **ask you
to declare any other assistance** then, so we understand your full picture.

**Is there a deadline?**
Not for this pilot — applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. We'll keep you
updated after you apply.

**What if I'm not shortlisted?**
We'll let you know by email. It doesn't stop you applying to a future round.

**Is there a cost to apply?**
No. Applying is free, and takes about 5–10 minutes.

**I have another question.**
Email **tamiliam@gmail.com** and we'll help.
